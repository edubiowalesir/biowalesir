About BioWaleSir

Built for Biology.
Built for India.

BioWaleSir is built with one clear purpose — to be the most reliable, complete, and free Biology resource for Class 9 to 12 students and teachers in India. Every note, diagram, question, and resource is carefully aligned with NCERT and CBSE patterns so students can rely on us completely — no need to look elsewhere.

Started by a passionate biology educator, BioWaleSir grew from a simple Google Drive folder of notes shared with students to a full platform. The goal was always the same — give every student access to quality biology content, whether they are in a metro city or a small town, with internet or without, completely free.

A small team of dedicated biology teachers and education enthusiasts who believe that quality education should be accessible to all. Our content is reviewed by experienced CBSE educators and updated regularly to match the latest syllabus and exam patterns.
